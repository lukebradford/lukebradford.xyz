GLOSSOLOGY


Luke Bradford


On a wild journey from stars to sea to land, Glossology unearths the hidden personalities of dozens of animals and the planet itself, reveling in the joy of nature and the materiality of language. The book is a set of lipograms: texts that avoid certain letters of the alphabet. Each poem is a definition of a term that uses only the letters in the term itself — living proof that constraints can be liberating, even when the limitations they impose are extraordinary.
