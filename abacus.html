ABACUS


Luke Bradford



Abacus is a collection of constrained poetry: each poem is governed by a different formal rule applied to its entire text. In some, the central constraint is obvious. In others, you might need to work to find it. I seek revenge on a world that is nowhere near as orderly as it ought to be.
